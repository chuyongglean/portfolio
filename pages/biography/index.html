01

The Narrative Journey

Born on 7 April 2005 in Babungo, Cameroon, my introduction to technology was forged in a climate of necessity and profound resilience. During the regional crises of 2018, I pivoted from idle observation to active digital mastery. My first experiments—basic HTML websites and complex affiliate marketing algorithms built on an entry-level iTel A33—were humble beginnings that planted the seed of a lifelong obsession with high-performance digital architecture.

Between 2018 and 2021, my path took a physical turn. I mastered technical trades that required the same mathematical precision I now apply to code: welding and intricate weaving. For seven years, weaving was my primary source of capital generation. It was more than a trade; it was a masterclass in pattern recognition and structural integrity. It taught me that complex systems are just the sum of single, intentional, and perfectly placed movements.

"The discipline of weaving taught me that the strongest structures are built through consistent, intentional logic—one thread at a time."

By 2022, I founded Shuyong Tech. I successfully scaled a mobile developer community to over 36,000 active members, mastering global APK distribution through Uptodown and other primary stores. This venture provided the technical and financial catalyst to transition into the Apple ecosystem and professional Flutter development, eventually leading to my official registration as Chuyong Glean Ngang.
